What ConnectForEdu does

ConnectForEdu ameliorates student loan debt for ambitious, driven students. We'll connect you with potential donors, comprising mostly alumni, organizations, and corporations, through a network of profiles. You'll attract traffic to your custom profiles via a blogging platform or other means, and then corporations will sponsor your page with advertisements at charitable rates. In addition to direct donations, a portion of the advertising revenue that you help generate goes directly toward paying off your loan debt.

Who we can help

We're just starting to experiment with ConnectForEdu, so we're looking for a first batch of eager, talented, and passionate students to bring on board. We'll screen applicants on certain criteria, such as financial aid eligibility, amount of student loan debt, past achievements, academic performance, and current extracurricular involvement.

Application Process

Since we're just starting out the program, there is no formal application process yet. Shoot us an email about yourself and we'll set up some time for a skype or phone conversation to see if you're a good fit for ConnectForEdu and if ConnectForEdu is a good fit for you.

Contact

Send an email to contact@connectforedu.com with your name, university, resume, and a few words about yourself. We'll get back to you as soon as possible!
