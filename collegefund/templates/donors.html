Student loan debt is a problem that you can help solve.

For students seeking a college education in America, it has become increasingly difficult to pursue a degree without amassing a large student loan debt. The average student in America graduates with between $25,000-$30,000 in student loans, and the total student loan debt in America has doubled just in the past five years, nearing $1 trillion. The staggering amount of loan debt that these college students graduate with becomes a tremendous barrier for students from low-income backgrounds, often forcing them to forgo their career aspirations.
