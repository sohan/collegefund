Mission Statement

Our mission is to help ameliorate student loan debt in America by connecting indebted college students with potential donors through an online network that helps them develop mutually beneficial relationships.

Values

Transparency
Absolute disclosure of information between our company, students and donors.
Choice (Ownership)
Donors have the choice to fund any student based on any criteria they want and students are encouraged to take full ownership of their donation and networking experience on our site.
Students first
The primary purpose of our organization is to help students be relieved of excessive financial burden. Therefore, everything we do (or don’t do) is considered with the student’s best interest in mind.
Integrity
All actions taken, or allowed, by our company will be carefully considered upon a foundation of ethical principles.
Equal Opportunity
Our company does not discriminate on any person’s race, gender, sexuality, religion, etc. Our company’s purpose is to create equality of opportunity among college students.
